Formula Open Modpack

This modpack is a version of the Open Modpack and was created by Fy17. Everyone who participates in the races needs to use the modpack to be visible. It’s also recommended for anyone watching, so you can see who’s leading the races.

The modpack shows a list of how many laps each driver has completed, how many pit stops they’ve made, and includes a live leaderboard displaying the race standings. There’s also an automatic timer to show lap times. Click the button below to download the modpack!
